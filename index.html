About Koeberl Farms

Koeberl Farms is a family operated farm located on the Renville-Kandiyohi County Line in the heart of Minnesota. We have been growing soy beans, sugar beets, and sweet corn for over half a century. Many of the products that you use in your day to day life begin their long journey to your table from our soil.
